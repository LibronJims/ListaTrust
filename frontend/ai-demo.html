How AI Calculates Trust Scores

Based on RFM Analysis (Recency, Frequency, Monetary):

  • Recency (30%) - How recent was their last activity?
  • Frequency (20%) - How often do they transact?
  • Monetary (40%) - Do they pay on time?
  • Active Debts (10%) - Penalty for too many active debts

Risk Levels:

🟢 HIGH (70-100) - Low risk, pays on time

🟡 MEDIUM (40-69) - Moderate risk, occasional late payments

🔴 LOW (0-39) - High risk, frequent late payments
